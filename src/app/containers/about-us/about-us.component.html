About Us

FreshontheGo Grocery, part of SV Home Products, is an online retailer providing a wide range of quality Indian groceries to its consumers. It has speciality items named SV Homemade products that offer Rice, Edible Oil, millets, spices, masala powders, beverages and various other grocery items that are 100% Preservative and Chemical free.

FreshontheGo introduced its consumers to the concept of online delivery at doorstep/lobby. It is Toronto's first scheduled grocery delivery chain and serves its customers since 2014. Today, FreshontheGo caters over 18 regions across GTA.

Over the years, FreshOntheGo has established itself as an Online-retailer that is committed to providing the best hassle free grocery shopping experience. With its impressive range of premium assortment at affordable prices, FreshontheGo delivers authentic Indian grocery. FreshontheGO brand positioning – makes organic food affordable – embodies its philosophy of delighting shoppers with the best preservative and chemical free products empowering shoppers to make healthy and hassle-free choices.

Save more with FreshOntheGrocery! We provide you with the lowest prices on all your grocery needs at your doorstep/lobby. Shop and get delivered your products across categories like Indian grocery, Health products, South Indian food and Pooja items.
