FAQs

How are the orders delivered and who delivers?

A 3rd party vendor has been contracted to perform your delivery service. In order to ensure that your delivery is successful, your participation is needed. Below are answers to commonly asked questions about Fresh On The Go Grocery deliveries.


When will my order be delivered?

In the shopping cart, you will receive an Estimated Delivery Date.Delivery may be preponed or postponed if the weather is poor on the day of delivery, this will be duly notified in our whatsapp group. Our system calculates this estimated date considering the total amount of time to process your order including: packaging, transit time to the delivery provider, and transit time to the delivery address.


What is transit time?

The amount of time it takes your order to leave our distribution center and arrive at the local delivery partner. If the delivery address provided is an apartment our delivery team will attempt to contact 5 mins earlier at the telephone number provided in your order. If you opt for delivery at the lobby you can arrive at the lobby to collect the item. Deliveries will be made between the hours of 8am and 11pm on the delivery day we have provided. The delivery team will make every effort to deliver within the time window provided. You can join our whatsapp group to get the delivery updates.

What happens if the delivery team is not able to reach you at the telephone number provided at the time of order?

If the delivery team is not able to contact during delivery, any one of below will happen

  1. If the residential unit is an apartment,ordered package would be placed at the flat/suite's-doorstep with an additional service charge.
  2. If the residential unit is house,ordered package would be placed at the doorstep with no additional cost.

Cancellation Policy

If you a requesting to cancel your order you must contact us before 12 hrs from the estimated delivery date. We will do our best to accommodate your request. Please note once your order has begun to process in our Distribution Centers we are unable to cancel until it has reached the local delivery provider. You will receive a full refund for the value of the products (including applicable tax).
